# Quick Custom Domain Setup for Turbo Answer Privacy Policy

## 🚀 FASTEST Setup (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Buy Domain + Hosting Bundle
**Recommended: Hostinger (Most Affordable)**
1. Go to https://hostinger.com
2. Choose "Web Hosting" → "Single Web Hosting" ($1.99/month)
3. During checkout, register new domain (e.g., turboanswer.com)
4. Complete purchase - you get domain + hosting in one package

### Step 2: Upload Files (2 Minutes)
1. Check email for hosting login details
2. Go to your hosting control panel (hPanel)
3. Click "File Manager"
4. Navigate to `public_html` folder
5. Upload these files:
   - `privacy-policy-website.html` → rename to `index.html`
   - `domain-configuration-files/htaccess.txt` → rename to `.htaccess`
   - `domain-configuration-files/robots.txt` → keep as `robots.txt`
   - `domain-configuration-files/sitemap.xml` → keep as `sitemap.xml`

### Step 3: Enable SSL (1 Minute)
1. In hPanel, go to "Security" → "SSL/TLS"
2. Click "Enable Free SSL"
3. Wait 5-10 minutes for activation

### Step 4: Test Website
1. Visit `https://yourdomain.com`
2. Should show your privacy policy
3. Check for green lock icon (SSL working)
