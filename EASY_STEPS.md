# Easy AI Widget - 3 Simple Steps

## Step 1: Copy
```html
<script src="https://turbo-answer-ai.uc.r.appspot.com/widget/turbo-widget.js"></script>
<script>TurboWidget.init();</script>
```

## Step 2: Paste
Add this code before `</body>` on your website.

## Step 3: Done!
Your website now has AI chat support.

## Want Your Brand Color?
Change `#3b82f6` to your color:
```html
<script>
TurboWidget.init({
    primaryColor: '#your-color'
});
</script>
```

## Popular Colors
- **Blue**: `#3b82f6`
- **Green**: `#10b981` 
- **Purple**: `#8b5cf6`
- **Red**: `#ef4444`
